Social Events @ ICIMSP 2027

ICIMSP features a thoughtfully curated social programme that encourages networking, collaboration, and informal engagement beyond the technical sessions. These events also offer a chance to experience the cultural richness of Bhubaneswar — the Temple City of India.

All registered attendees are warmly invited to join these memorable gatherings, included with your conference registration.

Cultural Tour – Temples of Bhubaneswar

Friday, 2 July 2027 | Afternoon/Evening (TBC)

A guided journey through Kalingan architectural splendour

  • Guided visits to renowned temples, including Lingaraj, Mukteshvara, and Rajarani
  • Insights into Kalingan architecture and regional history from expert guides
  • Relaxed setting for informal interactions with fellow participants from across the globe
  • Ample photography opportunities at heritage marvels

Did you know? Bhubaneswar is known as the "Temple City of India", with over 500 ancient temples showcasing exquisite Kalingan architecture. Our tour focuses on the most iconic masterpieces.
