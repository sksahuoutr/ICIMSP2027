Formatting

  • Springer LNCS format - Use official template
  • 6-8 pages including references & figures
  • PDF format - Max 10 MB file size
  • All fonts must be embedded
  • No transparent pixels in images
  • No PDF security protection

Submission

Microsoft CMT Portal cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICIMSP2027
  • Enter all authors accurately
  • Follow Springer LNCS template
  • Remove identifying info for blind review
  • One author must register upon acceptance

Ethics & Policies

  • Originality: Original & unpublished work only
  • Plagiarism: All submissions will be checked
  • Authorship: All authors must have contributed
  • Conflicts: Must be disclosed during submission
  • Multiple Submissions: Not permitted

Proceedings Publication

  • Publisher: Springer LNCS
  • Indexing: Scopus, DBLP, Google Scholar, EI-Compendex, SCImago
  • Status: Subjected to final approval by Springer
  • All accepted & presented papers will be published

Extra Page Charges

  • Indian Authors: ₹1,770 per extra page beyond 6 pages
  • International Authors: $40 per extra page beyond 6 pages
  • Maximum allowed pages: 8 pages total
  • Charges apply only after acceptance

Additional Guidelines

  • Papers must be written in clear English
  • Use SI units for measurements
  • Figures must be clear and readable
  • Tables should have proper headings
  • References must follow Springer LNCS style
