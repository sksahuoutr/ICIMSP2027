Author Guidelines for ICIMSP 2027

All submissions to ICIMSP 2027 must follow these rules. Download templates and check formatting and policy before uploading your manuscript.

LaTeX Template Word Template Submission Link Details

Preparation & Formatting

  • Use official conference template (LaTeX/Word)
  • Length: max 12 pages incl. references, figures, tables
  • Submit in PDF format, max size: 10MB
  • All fonts must be scalable and embedded
  • Images: No transparent pixels/alpha channels
  • No PDF document security/protection

Submission Instructions

  • Submit manuscript via CMT portal (see link above)
  • Check page limit, formatting, PDF compatibility
  • Enter all authors’ names and affiliations accurately
  • Remove embedded metadata that could violate double-blind review if required
  • Fix any font, reference, or figure issues before upload

Cautions & Ethical Policy

  • No simultaneous/concurrent submission to other conferences/journals
  • No plagiarism or self-plagiarism
  • All co-authors must approve manuscript and submission
  • Late submissions are not accepted
  • Papers violating policy will be rejected without review

For questions on formatting or upload issues, contact ICIMSP 2027 organizers.
