Formatting Requirements

  • Use official conference template (Word/LaTeX) - Springer LNCS format
  • Minimum 6 pages, Maximum 8 pages including references, figures, tables
  • Submit in PDF format, max size 10 MB
  • All fonts must be scalable and embedded
  • No transparent pixels in images
  • No PDF security/protection enabled
  • Extra page charges: ₹1,770 per page (India) / $40 per page (International) beyond 6 pages

Submission Process

Submit via Microsoft CMT Portal:
https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/ICIMSP2027
  • Enter all authors' names and affiliations accurately
  • Ensure manuscript follows Springer LNCS template
  • Remove any identifying information for blind review (if applicable)
  • Fix font, reference, or figure issues before upload
  • At least one author must register for the conference upon acceptance

Ethics & Policies

  • Originality: Papers must be original and unpublished
  • Plagiarism: All submissions will be checked for plagiarism
  • Authorship: All listed authors must have contributed significantly
  • Conflicts of Interest: Must be disclosed during submission
  • Multiple Submissions: Not allowed
