Formatting Guidelines

  • Page Limit: Minimum 6 pages, Maximum 8 pages (including references)
  • Format: Springer LNCS (Lecture Notes in Computer Science)
  • Font: 10pt Times New Roman or LaTeX default
  • Spacing: As per Springer LNCS template
  • Margins: As per Springer LNCS template
  • Figures/Tables: Properly captioned and referenced
  • References: Springer LNCS format
  • Extra Pages: ₹1,770 per page (India) / $40 per page (International)

Springer LNCS Format

  • All accepted papers will be published in Springer LNCS (subject to final approval)
  • Authors must follow the Springer LNCS template strictly

  • Maximum 8 pages allowed (6 pages minimum)

Important Notes

  • Use the template as-is — do not modify style definitions
  • Ensure all fonts are embedded when converting to PDF
  • Remove any document protection before submission
  • Verify images have no transparent pixels
  • Check PDF file size does not exceed 10 MB
  • Papers must be original and unpublished
  • At least one author must register for the conference
