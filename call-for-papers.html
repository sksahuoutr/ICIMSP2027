Invitation

The School of Electronic Sciences, OUTR invites original research contributions for the International Conference on Intelligent Machine and Signal Processing (ICIMSP 2027), to be held in Hybrid format at Bhubaneswar from 1–3 July 2027.

ICIMSP 2027 focuses on innovation at the intersection of Intelligent Machines, Autonomous Systems, and Advanced Signal & Image Processing.

Proceedings Publication: ICIMSP 2027 proceedings will be published in Springer LNCS (Subjected to Final Approval).

Student Paper Contest

Eligibility: UG/PG/Ph.D students. Original/poster work.

Categories & Prizes

  • B.E./B.Tech/M.Sc Poster: 1st ₹5,000, 2nd ₹3,000
  • M.E./M.Tech/M.Phil/PhD Poster: 1st ₹6,000, 2nd ₹4,000

Guidelines

  • Max 2 authors per paper
  • Manuscript: 8 pages (Springer Nature format)
  • Submit PDF, Biography, Publications
  • Key Dates: Submission 15 Jul–15 Sep 2026

Evaluation & Awards

  • Expert review panel
  • Poster round and interactive session
  • Awards: Cash & certificate
