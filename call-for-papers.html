Invitation

The School of Electronic Sciences, OUTR invites original research contributions for the International Conference on Intelligent Machine and Signal Processing (ICIMSP 2027), held in Hybrid format at Bhubaneswar from 1–3 July 2027.

ICIMSP 2027 focuses on innovation at the intersection of intelligent machines, autonomous systems, and advanced signal & image processing.
