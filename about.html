About OUTR

The State Government established the Odisha University of Technology and Research located at Bhubaneswar by upgrading the College of Engineering & Technology, Bhubaneswar through the Odisha University of Technology and Research Act, 2021 (known as Odisha Act 17 of 2021). The University started functioning with effect from 8th October, 2021 through Notification No. SDTE-HTE-HTE-II-0012-2021/4578 dated 08.10.2021 of SD&TE Department, Government of Odisha.

The First Statutes 2022 of the University was notified vide Notification No. SDTE-HTE-HTE-II-0012-2022/1075 dated 09.02.2023. The University is governed by the Odisha University of Technology and Research Act, 2021 and its First Statutes, 2022.

OUTR is committed to fostering excellence in technical education, research, and innovation, serving as a hub for cutting-edge technological advancements in the region and beyond.

About ICIMSP 2027

The International Conference on Intelligent Machine and Signal Processing (ICIMSP 2027) is a premier forum that brings together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to share recent advances in intelligent systems, machine learning, signal processing, embedded technologies, and allied interdisciplinary fields.

The conference aims to promote innovation, collaboration, and knowledge exchange through keynote lectures, technical paper presentations, and networking opportunities, creating a vibrant platform for discussing emerging challenges and future directions in intelligent machine and signal processing research.

ICIMSP 2027 will be held in hybrid mode, welcoming both in-person and virtual participants to engage with global experts and peers. The event features renowned keynote speakers, industry panels, paper presentations, and student contests.

Key Policies

  • 1. Mandatory Registration: At least one author per accepted paper must register for inclusion in the proceedings and technical program (submission to IEEE Xplore / Springer Nature subject to approval).
  • 2. Paper Presentation: At least one author must present the paper. No-show papers will be excluded from the proceedings.
  • 3. Refund Policy: Registration fees are non-refundable. No cancellations allowed.
  • 4. Multiple Papers: For two papers by the same author, an additional 50% of the base registration fee will be charged. Policies for more than two papers will be communicated separately.
  • 5. Extra Pages: ₹1770 per page (India) / $40 per page (International) beyond 6 pages (maximum 8 pages allowed).
  • 6. Verification: Students and academicians must provide a valid institute ID. IEEE/IAPR members must provide a valid membership card.
  • 7. Certificates & Receipts: Presentation certificates will be issued to presenting authors. Payment receipts will be provided upon successful registration.
